## Purpose

Contains your **notes from reading and analyzing research papers**. Each paper gets its own subdirectory with individual notes for each section. The goal is critical engagement, not just summarization.

## What Belongs Here

- One subdirectory per paper, named after the paper title
- Section-by-section breakdowns (Abstract.md, Introduction.md, Methods.md, etc.)
- Key takeaways, questions, and connections to other work

## Naming Conventions

- Directory: Paper title (e.g., `Attention Is All You Need/`)
- Files: Section names (e.g., `Abstract.md`, `Introduction.md`)
