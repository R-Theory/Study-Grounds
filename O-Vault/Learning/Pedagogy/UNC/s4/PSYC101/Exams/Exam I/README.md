# Exam I (PSYC101)

> Self-contained study hub for PSYC101 Exam I — all preparation materials in one place.

## Purpose

Contains everything needed to prepare for PSYC101 Exam I. The master study guide provides the central summary, with detailed chapter notes and active recall resources in subdirectories.

## What Belongs Here

- `Exam_I_Master_Study_Guide.md` — Central comprehensive summary
- `notes/` — Chapter-by-chapter detailed notes
- `resources/my-resources/` — Flashcards, Anki imports, and study aids

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- Instructor-provided exam resources (those stay in the repo at `course-repos/PSYC101/Exams/`)
- Materials for other exams (each exam gets its own directory)
