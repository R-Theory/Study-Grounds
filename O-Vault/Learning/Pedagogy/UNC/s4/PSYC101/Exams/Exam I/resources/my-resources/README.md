## Purpose

Contains **your self-created study aids** designed for active recall and review. These complement the detailed notes by providing tools for testing your knowledge.

## What Belongs Here

- Flashcard sets (Markdown-based, general or per-chapter)
- Anki import files (`.txt` formatted for Anki)
- AI-generated study aids (NotebookLM prompts, etc.)
- Practice problem sets you've created

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- Detailed chapter notes (those go in `notes/`)
- Instructor-provided resources (those stay in the repo)

## Naming Conventions

- Flashcards: `Flashcards.md` (general) or `Flashcards_Chapter#.md` (per-chapter)
- Anki imports: `PSYC101_Exam1_Anki_Import.txt`
